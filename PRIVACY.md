# Privacy Policy — Zoom Toolkit

_Last updated: 2026-06-24_

Zoom Toolkit is a desktop application that runs entirely on your own computer.

## What data we collect

**None.** Zoom Toolkit does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It has no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, and no network communication.

## What the app stores locally

- **Your preferences** (language, last-used folders, file types, threshold, calendar and sorting options) are saved on your computer at `%APPDATA%\ZoomToolkit\config.json`.
- **Operation logs** are written, at your direction, to the output folder you choose.

These files never leave your device. You can delete them at any time.

## File access

The app only reads, copies, and moves audio files in the folders **you explicitly select** within the app. It does not access any other files.
